Welcome to Rubbish!

Welcome to Rubbish, a website dedicated for the environment. The Environment, the place that we see around us and where we all live in, is constantly at risk of being destroyed by our actions and carelessness. This site will show the different causes and effects of pollution and what solutions and preventive measures we can do to prevent further damage to our already dying world.



About the Site

This is a solo project that aims to help spread awareness on pollution, its causes and effects, and the different solutions and preventions one can make to reduce the growing damage we are doing to the planet. To learn more about the site, click on the link below:

Water Pollution

The water that we see and use today is not only H2O, but it is also one important component in our life. We use it to drink, bathe, wash, do recreational activities, and more. However, due to our own actions, the water becomes contaminated and as a result, it is no longer useful in our daily life, sometimes even harmful.
