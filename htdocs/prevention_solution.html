Solutions and Prevention

Land Pollution

Solutions and Prevention of Land Pollution:

  • Reduce the use of chemicals;
  • Plant trees;
  • Avoid plastic;
  • Avoid littering;
  • Cleaning programs;
  • Proper Waste Disposal and;
  • Educating people about Land Pollution


Water Pollution

Solutions and Prevention of Water Pollution:

  • Avoid disposing waste to bodies of water;
  • Proper Waste Disposal;
  • Cleaning programs near bodies of water and;
  • Educating people about Water Pollution


Air Pollution

Solutions and Prevention of Air Pollution:

  • Avoid the use of vehicles that emit smoke;
  • Reduce the use of aerosols and;
  • Educating people about Air Pollution
